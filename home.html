About us

Our motive is to create a positive space where people can unwind and relax. Poor mental health can affect the normal functioning of a person's life. We want to promote an optimistic approach toward mental health and help people manage their emotions in an efficient way that would help them live happier life. Our goal is to promote overall mental wellness.
